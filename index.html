Principal's Message

Dear Parents, Students, and Staff, Welcome to Benson Preparatory School! It is with great pleasure and enthusiasm that I extend a warm welcome to each and every one of you as we embark on another exciting academic year. At Benson Prep, our mission is to provide a nurturing and inclusive learning environment where every student can thrive academically, socially, and emotionally. We are committed to fostering a love for learning, promoting critical thinking skills, and instilling values of respect, responsibility, and resilience in our students. As the principal, I am dedicated to ensuring that each student receives the support and guidance they need to reach their full potential. Our team of experienced educators is passionate about creating engaging and enriching learning experiences that inspire curiosity and creativity. We believe that education is a partnership between the school, parents, and the community. Together, we can create a strong foundation for our students to succeed not only in the classroom but also in their future endeavors. I encourage you to actively participate in your child's education and to communicate openly with our staff. Together, we can make a positive difference in the lives of our students and help them achieve their dreams. I am excited about the opportunities that lie ahead for our school community, and I look forward to working closely with each of you throughout the school year.

Warm regards, Dr. James Findley Principal, Benson Preparatory School
